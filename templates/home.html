Gist. is a simple and easy to use webapp that lets you connet with your friends and share your thoughts through tweets and share events.

Tweet

Share your stories with your friends. Let them know whats new in your life.

Plan

Quickly schedule meetings and events, also send it your friends so that they can join too.

Connect

Connect with friends, family and other people you know.

Tweets

Tweets are very simple and quick way to share your thoughts with your friends. Use emojis to make them more attractive. Your tweets can only be seen by your friends.


Events

You can easily schedule your upcoming meetings and events. You can edit them it your will and also share it with your friends so that they can also join.


Connect

Connet with your friends by send them friend requests. Once accepted you can view their tweets and vice-versa. You can also share your events with one another.
